Nihilus new album is out this April.
Nihilus is a tech death metal band based in bangalore, India.
They were formed in 2010 and have an EP named "Without Grey Matter", which was released back in 2015.
Nihilus band members: Manu (Guitar), Pearl (Vocals), Karthik (Bass), Siddharth (Drums)
Past members of Nihilus: Navneet (Vocals), Arunav (Vocals), Jason (Bass)
